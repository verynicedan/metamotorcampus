Why MetaMotor Campus?

Unique Selling Point

  • No headset sales or external access to protect IP and exclusivity.
  • Customized data collection – we build and upload all simulation data with the help of automotive experts and instructional designers.
  • Content is continuously updated to reflect latest technologies and repair techniques.
  • Employer & Industry Partnerships: Partner with local workshops, dealerships, and automotive chains for:
    • On-the-job placements
    • Custom training for workshop staff
    • Direct talent pipeline from your center
  • Supports green mobility training (EV, hybrid modules)
  • Promotes reskilling and upskilling (SDG 4: Quality Education)
  • Real-time shadowing of global experts, offers live VR link to actual master technicians. Trainees can “enter” their workspace in VR and follow every movement.
  • AI Coaching – AI mechanic avatar in VR guides users based on their learning progress (tier)

Why It Stands Out?

  • Safe practice: Learners can work on virtual engines or electrical systems without damaging real parts.
  • Repeatable scenarios: Train on rare or dangerous car faults as often as needed.
  • Cost-saving: Reduces need for physical vehicles or tools for every learner.
  • Realistic environments: Simulates workshop conditions, boosting confidence and job readiness before stepping into the field.
