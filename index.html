Why MetaMotor Campus?

Unique Selling Point

  • No headset sales or external access to protect IP and exclusivity.
  • Customized data collection – we build and upload all simulation data with the help of automotive experts and instructional designers.
  • Content is continuously updated to reflect latest technologies and repair techniques.
  • Employer & Industry Partnerships:
    • On-the-job placements
    • Custom training for workshop staff
    • Direct talent pipeline from your center
  • Supports green mobility training (EV, hybrid modules)
  • Promotes reskilling and upskilling (SDG 4: Quality Education)
  • Real-time shadowing of global experts via VR to follow master technicians in their workspaces.
  • AI Coaching – mechanic avatar that adapts guidance to user skill levels.

Why It Stands Out?

  • Safe practice: Work on virtual engines/electrical systems risk-free.
  • Repeatable scenarios: Train on rare or high-risk faults multiple times.
  • Cost-saving: Less reliance on physical vehicles/tools per learner.
  • Realistic environments: Simulated workshops boost confidence before job entry.
